Design principles honoured on this page

claude-design-prompt · speed check
01
Typography off the block-list
Space Grotesk for prose, IBM Plex Mono for labels. No Inter, no Roboto, no Arial, no system-ui.
02
oklch, no rainbow gradients
All colour tokens declared as oklch() with one accent hue. Gradients, if any, stay within 30° of the same hue.
03
Namespaced tokens
Every CSS variable is prefixed --lab-. No risk of collision when inlined into a larger host page.
04
Tweaks protocol ready
Listener registered before __edit_mode_available is posted. Defaults wrapped in EDITMODE-BEGIN/END.
05
No placeholder filler
Counts and stack strings reflect the real files on disk. No made-up stats, no decorative emoji, no hand-drawn SVG.
06
Hit targets ≥ 44px
Every clickable element — nav link, card CTA — reserves at least 44px of tap area per mobile accessibility guidance.
